New GST Registration Checklist

Documents required, eligibility criteria, and processing timelines.

Who needs GST registration?

  • Turnover exceeds threshold or inter‑state supply
  • E‑commerce sellers
  • Casual taxable persons

Documents

  • PAN, Aadhaar
  • Business proof (incorporation/partnership or shop act)
  • Address proof (rent agreement/utility bill)
  • Bank proof (cancelled cheque)

Timeline

Typically 2–5 working days after complete document submission.
